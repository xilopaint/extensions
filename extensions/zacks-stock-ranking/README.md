# Zacks.com

Zacks.com is the website for Zacks Investment Research, an independent investment research firm known for its proprietary Zacks Rank, a stock rating system (1=Strong Buy to 5=Strong Sell) based on earnings estimate revisions.

## Commands

Zacks – Enter a ticker directly and get quick stock information including the Zacks Rank.

Zacks Search – Search for a company and hit return to get the Zacks info.

Zacks Recents – See a list of your recent Zacks searches.
